Notgios offers (or will at some point) near zero configuration of Linux based cloud servers, and offers the following services:

  • Monit-like supervision of processes.
  • Collection of process metrics (CPU, memory, IO usage).
  • Collection of disk (total and per directory) usage metrics.
  • Collection of general system metrics (load average, total CPU, total IO).
  • Easy visualization of said metrics.
  • Traditional alarm functionality (text messages, emails) based on metric thresholds.
  • Predictive alarm functionality based on configurable trends.
